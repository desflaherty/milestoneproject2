Dublin Fire Brigade Station Areas

Displays Fire Station areas by the
total volume of call outs over the years 2013-2015. Click on a bar in the chart to filter the data for that area.

Fire Stations with Highest Call Out Incidents

Displays the 'Top 10' Fire Station areas by the total volume of call outs over the years 2013-2015. Click on a bar in the chart to filter the data for that area.

Top 10 Incidents by Description

Displays 'Top 10' Incident Type by call out. You can use the 'Type Of Call Out' dropdown on the top menu to filter on 'Fire' or 'Special Service' and the Top 10 for each will be displayed.

All Description Types

Displays all the Incidents recorded over the years 2013-2015.

Time Line of Incidents from January 2013 To December 2015

Displays a timeline of all the Incidents recorded over the years 2013-2015. You can zoom in on the chart using the mouse or hover over a particular date to see Incident volume.

Time Line of Incidents from January 2013 To December 2015 - Split by Service Type

Displays a timeline of all the Incidents recorded over the years 2013-2015 split by Service Type. You can zoom in on the chart using the mouse or hover over a particular date to see Incident volume.

Time of Incident Calls - 24 Hour

Displays 'Time of Call' to the nearest hour. For example any calls from 20:00 to 21:00 will be displayed under 20:00, the count shows the calls over the following hour.

Day of Incident Calls - Ordered by Highest Volume

Displays the total amount of Incidents by day of the week ordered by the highest occurrence of Incidents. You can select a particular day to filter graphs by that day only.
